Subject: Outsourced Accounting / Controller Services

Harbor Point Nonprofit Alliance, a US not-for-profit (~$12M annual budget), is looking to outsource its day-to-day accounting function. Their controller is retiring and they have decided not to backfill the role internally. They need ongoing transaction processing, monthly close, financial reporting to the board, grant fund tracking, and controller-level oversight on a recurring monthly basis. This is an ongoing operate-and-run engagement, not a one-time project. They want a partner to own the accounting function going forward, starting within 60 days of the controller's departure.
