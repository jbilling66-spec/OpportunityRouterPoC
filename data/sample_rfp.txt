REQUEST FOR PROPOSAL — Enterprise Cloud ERP Implementation

Organization: Meridian Energy Holdings (US-headquartered, ~$4.2B revenue, energy sector)

Meridian seeks a qualified professional-services firm to select, design, and implement a
new cloud-based ERP to replace its aging on-premise financial and supply-chain systems.

Scope:
- Lead platform selection and target-state design
- Configure and build finance, procurement, and supply-chain modules
- Migrate and reconcile data from legacy systems
- Stand up the system to production with a target go-live within 14 months

This is a net-new implementation; no platform has been selected yet, though the program is
funded with executive sponsorship. Proposals due within 45 days.
